Summary

Alibaba (BABA) is a mega-cap e-commerce company based in China. The company operates two largest B2B and B2C platforms in China and accounts for more than half of the domestic e-commerce market. The company is also well-known for its ownership in other companies such as Ant Finance, Cainiao, and Ali Cloud.

And we strongly recommend a buy on BABA with the target price of 251 USD/share, or 26% upside as of this writing. Rationales for this recommendation include 1) the strong network effect where the core is two biggest e-commerce platform in China, 2) a healthy growth in sales which is favored by the expansion of the e-commerce industry in China and the acquisition strategy of the company 3)potentials of Ali Cloud in the cloud computing industry.

Besides detailed business and market insights to support the above-mentioned investment rationales, the report also touches upon the potential effects of Covid-19 on the short-term growth as well as risks the company is facing.
