Introduction

Fundamental analysis is a valuation method that estimates the fair share price of a public company based on its intrinsic value. In this project, we estimate the fair share price of Beyond Meat and Alibaba, using DCF and comparable methods. Despite thorough research and careful modeling work, there are great uncertainties involved in the predicted fair prices of these companies due to the unprecedented effects of Covid-19 on the global economy as well as the companiesâ€™ operation.
